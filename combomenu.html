Combo Menu Program

Purpose: The combo menu project is used to order a meal (burger, fries, drink). The user is told the price of the items, orders the type of burger they want, as well as the size of their beverage and fries if they want them. The user also orders the amount of ketchup packets they want for their burger. If they order all items in the meal, they get a one dollar discount. The total cost of their order is then added up and given to the user. This applies to real life because if someone wants to order lunch or dinner for themselves, they can use this.

In iteration 1, The user is asked which type of burger they would like, based on the three choices, hamburger, cheeseburger, and bacon cheeseburger. Whichever burger is chosen, the price of that burger is added to the total price of the order. After the burger is chosen, the output will display the burger chosen and its price. Variables included: price - the total price of the order, burger - the burger chosen
